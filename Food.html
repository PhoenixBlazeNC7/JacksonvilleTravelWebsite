Food



There are many amazing restaurants to eat at in Jacksonville and these are on this list because they are special, delicious, and have something about them that you won't forget.

Poes Tavern



Poes Tavern is a restaurant by the beach themed after famous poet Edgar Allen Poe. The menu is themed and named in reference to many of his more famous works. Even the bathroom walls are decorated with pages from his stories. It is open from 11 am to 10 pm every day of the week. It is located at 363 Atlantic Blvd, Atlantic Beach, FL 32233.

Cinottis Bakery



Cinottis Bakery is a small family-owned bakery and sandwich shop in Jacksonville. They serve donuts, cakes, cupcakes, sandwhiches, and more! It is popular amongst the locals espeacially for it's themed cookies whenever holidays or important events are coming up.

Salt Life Food Shack



Salt Life Food Shack is a surfing and fishing themed themed seafood hangout by the beach. They have evrything from sushi to tacos to burgers. The Salt Life brand includes not only the food shack, but also surfing and fishing attire. There are four locations of the restraunt, all in Florida. The flagship restraunt is the one in Jacksonville. There is also a Salt Life clothing store down the road.

Fogo De Chão



Fogo De Chão is an upscale brazillian steakhouse chain. They have all you can eat tableside carved meat and a large salad bar. The founders of Fogo de Chão grew up on a traditional Southern Brazilian farm in the Serra Gaucha and here they learned to cook in the churrasco grilling tradition that they would later expand into a massive scale restaurant operation.

The Happy Grilled Cheese



The Happy Grilled Cheese is a food truck and has two dine in restaurant locations. They serve a wide variety of grilled cheeses. All of the locations are popular with the locals and a welcome sight when its time for lunch.
