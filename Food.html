Food



There are many amazing restaurants to eat at in Jacksonville and these are on this list because they are speacial, delicious, and have something about them that you won't forget.

Poes Tavern



Poes Tavern is a restraunt by the beach themed after famous poet Edgar Allen Poe. The menu is themed and named in reference to many of his more famous works. Even the bathroom walls are decorated with pages from his stories. It is open from 11 am to 10 pm every day of the week. It is located at 363 Atlantic Blvd, Atlantic Beach, FL 32233.

Cinottis Bakery



Cinottis Bakery is a small family-owned bakery and sandwich shop in Jacksonville. They serve donuts, cakes, cupcakes, sandwhiches, and more! It is popular amongst the locals espeacially for it's themed cookies whenever holidays or important events are coming up.
