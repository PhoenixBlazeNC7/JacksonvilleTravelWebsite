Places to Go


Adventure Landing



Adventure Landing is an arcade and waterpark located in Jacksonville, Florida. It is located at 1944 Beach Blvd, Jacksonville Beach, FL 32250, United States. It is a popular place to go for family and friends. Inside the arcade, they have games, laser tag, 2 bowling lanes, a flight simulator and more!. There is even a candy store located inside the building. Outside, there is an aligator pond where you can pay to feed the alligators.
https://jacksonville-beach.adventurelanding.com


The Beach



Jacksonville has many beautiful beaches that all have their own charm. The one thing they all have in common is that they are amazing places to spend a few hours.

Cummer Art Museum



The Cummer Art Museum is full of wonderful works of art from a wide variety of artists. From impressionism to the Renassaince, this museum has it all. Even the gardens on the ground are magnificent and breathtaking. The museum is located at 829 Riverside Ave, Jacksonville, FL 32204, United States.
https://www.cummermuseum.org


Top Golf



TopGolf is a sports entertainment center where friends and family go to play a variety of games centering around golf. The center is located at 10531 Brightman Blvd, Jacksonville, FL 32246, United States. In house chefs can prepare anything from burgers to flatbreads to donut holes. The bar is also very popular. It is a popular place among people of all ages. There is fun for everyone.
https://topgolf.com/us/jacksonville/


iFlyJacksonville



iFlyJacksonville is an indoor sky diving center located at 10579 Brightman Blvd, Jacksonville, FL 32246, United States. It allows you to experience the thrill of skydiving in a safe and controlled enviornment. They often host field trips and birthday parties. It is widley popular with children and adults alike.
https://www.iflyworld.com/jacksonville/


Jacksonville Zoo and Gardens



The Jacksonville Zoo and Gardens is a beautiful place full of exotic and interesting plants and animals. They have a wide variety of animals ranging from komodo dragons to tigers to elephants. It is located at 370 Zoo Pkwy, Jacksonville, FL 32218, United States.
https://www.jacksonvillezoo.org


Main Event



Main Event is an arcade and entertainment center with an arcade, a bowling alley, and a laser tag arena. It has 20 bowling lanes and over 100 games. They serve a variety of food and drinks. It is located at 10370 Philips Hwy, Jacksonville, FL 32256, United States.
https://www.mainevent.com/locations/florida/jacksonville/


Beach Bowl



Beach Bowl is a bowling alley located at jax beach. It was a very popular bowling alley that went under construction a few years ago and after millions of dollars in renovations, it has reopened bigger and better than ever. It has 24 bowling lanes, VIP party rooms, three full bars, a restaurant serving up all of your favorites, an arcade, and a second floor patio. It is located at 818 Beach Blvd, Jacksoville Beach, FL 32250, United States.
https://www.beachbowljax.com


Osprey Challenge Course



The Osprey Challenge Course is a rope course located at the University of North Florida campus on 11884 UNF Dr., Jacksonville, FL 32224, USA. It is used both for fun and as a team building activity.
https://www.unf.edu/recwell/ecoadventure/occ.html


Museum of Science & History



The Jacksonville Museum of Science & History is a private non-profit located on the Southbank Riverwalk. It has a variety of exhibits and events that rotate every few months.
https://themosh.org


St. Johns Town Center Shopping Mall



The St. Johns Town Center is a shopping mall owned by the Simon mall corporation. It has a wide variety of clothing stores, appliance stores, tech stores, game stores and more. While there you can do everything from buy a new wardrobe to completing an escape room to discovering your new favorite book series.
https://www.simon.com/mall/st-johns-town-center
