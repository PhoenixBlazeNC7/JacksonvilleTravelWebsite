Places to Go


Adventure Landing

Adventure Landing is an arcade and waterpark located in Jacksonville, Florida. It is located at 1944 Beach Blvd, Jacksonville Beach, FL 32250, United States. It is a popular place to go for family and friends. Inside the arcade, they have games, laser tag, 2 bowling lanes, a flight simulator and more!. There is even a candy store located inside the building. Outside, there is an aligator pond where you can pay to feed the alligators.
